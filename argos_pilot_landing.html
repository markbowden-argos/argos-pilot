This pilot is designed to give your organisation a hands-on evaluation of Geotab's fleet management platform, deployed on a representative sample of your fleet. Over the course of the pilot, Argos Connected Solutions will work alongside your team to configure the platform to your operational requirements, provide training, and deliver data-driven insights to support a confident purchasing decision.

Key Objectives

What success looks like at the end of this pilot

  • 01
    Real-Time Vehicle Visibility Demonstrate live GPS tracking accuracy and asset utilisation dashboards across all pilot vehicles.

    Predictive Maintenance Alerts Validate engine diagnostic (OBD) data and fault code alerts to quantify potential downtime reduction.
